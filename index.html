Our Story

More Than A Gym.
A Complete Wellness Destination.

Safari Gym & Spa lies in the heart of Bahria Town, Rawalpindi — located in Safari Mall Basement, Safari Villas 1. We offer everything from world-class gym equipment to spa treatments, sauna, massage therapy, yoga, aerobics, and a dedicated ladies-only gym. Whatever your fitness or wellness goal, we have it all under one roof.
